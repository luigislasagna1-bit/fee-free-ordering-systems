## Full description (App Store ~4000 / Play max 4000)

**Run your restaurant's online orders from one app — and keep 100% of every sale.**

Fee Free Ordering turns your phone or tablet into the command center for your online
orders. Customers order from your own branded page (pickup, delivery, dine-in, and
catering) at **0% commission**, and every order lands here the instant it's placed.

**Never miss an order**
• New orders ring a loud, repeating alert — even with the screen off.
• If an order sits unaccepted, the app can call your phone so nothing slips.
• Accept, prep, and complete orders from one clean screen.

**Print without the headaches**
• Print kitchen tickets and customer receipts directly to your WiFi thermal printer
  (Star, Epson, Bixolon and other ESC/POS printers) — no extra hardware or cloud print
  service required.
• Set how many kitchen and customer copies print per order.

**Everything your front-of-house needs**
• Table reservations and reserve-then-order pre-orders.
• Scheduled / advance orders for catering and busy days.
• Live order list with prep times, customer details, and order history.

**Part of a complete, commission-free platform**
Fee Free Ordering gives your restaurant its own branded ordering page, a customer
database you own, promotions, and marketing tools — all designed so first-time
customers become direct, repeat customers. Available in 38 languages. Proudly built
in Canada.

Free to start. Add only the paid extras you want (online card payments, a hosted
website, multi-location, and more). No commission on your direct orders, ever.

Questions? 24/7 support at 1-888-618-8765 or support@feefreeordering.com.
