## A. Kitchen — Reject a pending reservation  (071dacf)
**Setup** 🛎️ Reservations settings → Auto-confirm **OFF**.
1. 📱 Make a table-only booking.
2. 🧑‍🍳 It appears **pending** + rings the alarm. Open it → **Accept** + **Reject** (+ Print).
3. **Reject** → booking leaves; customer gets a **declined** email (sender = restaurant name).
4. Repeat → **Accept** → customer gets **confirmed** email; detail now shows **Seated / No-show**.
5. With Auto-confirm OFF, a new booking sends only a "request received" email until you Accept.
